Team Information

The Norwich Testing Initiative (NTI) is a newly formed initiative which will launch a large-scale screening pilot for SARS-CoV-2, the virus which causes the COVID-19 disease. The pilot begins on Monday 4 January 2021 and runs for four weeks.
