Testing and Tracing

If you have experienced/developed symptoms of coronavirus or have been in contact with someone who has, you must self isolate and sign up to book a test here.

You must self-isolate if ANY of the following applies to you:

New and persistent cough
Elevated temperature (head, back, armpits, chest) above 37.2°C
You have been contacted by NHS Track and Trace
You have been in contact with someone who has developed symptoms or recieved a positive test in the last 7 days
Any of the above applies to a member of your household
